📚 Quick Guide

  • Admin: Create users (parents, drivers, bus minders), manage buses, and make assignments
  • Parent: Login to view your children's bus information and attendance status
  • Driver: Login to see children assigned to your bus and their status
  • Bus Minder: Login to mark attendance for children on your assigned bus

🔧 Testing Instructions

  • Start by going to Admin Dashboard
  • Create a Parent (with children), Driver, and Bus Minder
  • Go to Assignments to assign drivers/bus minders/children to buses
  • Use the generated credentials to login as different users
  • Test the full workflow: attendance marking, parent viewing, etc.
